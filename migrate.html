DNF migrate Plugin¶

Description¶

Migrates yum’s history, group and yumdb data to dnf.

Synopsis¶

dnf migrate [--nogroups] [--nohistory] [--noyumdb]

Options¶

dnf migrate

--nogroups
Do not migrate groups data.
--nohistory
Do not migrate history data.
--noyumdb
Do not migrate yumdb data.
