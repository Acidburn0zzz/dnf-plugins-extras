DNF migrate Plugin¶

Description¶

Migrates yum’s history, group and yumdb data to dnf.

Synopsis¶

dnf migrate [all|history|groups|yumdb]

Commands¶

dnf migrate [all]
Migrate all the data.
dnf migrate history
Migrate only the history.
dnf migrate groups
Migrate only the groups.
dnf migrate yumdb
Migrate only the yumdb.
